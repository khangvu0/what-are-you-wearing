About Us

Established in 2025, "What R U Wearing?" is a dedicated non-profit organization focused on tackling the environmental challenges posed by the fast fashion industry. Through our initiatives, we strive to promote responsible consumer behavior and advocate for environmentally friendly practices across various sectors. We conduct workshops, campaigns, and outreach programs that promote mindfulness in consumption and emphasize the importance of selecting environmentally friendly clothing options. Join us in our mission to redefine the fashion landscape and endorse responsible choices!

Mission & Values

Our mission is to raise awareness of the impacts of environmental issues within communities and to promote a global understanding that encourages sustainable practices. We aim to educate and collaborate with other non-profits that are working to facilitate these changes. We value integrity and innovation in addressing community needs, sharing knowledge with locals through educational events and donations.
